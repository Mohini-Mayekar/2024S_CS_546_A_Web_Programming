Delhi Diaries: A Day of Heritage, Culture, and Culinary Delights

December 15, 2018

Delhi, a city steeped in history and culture, offers a captivating journey through its bustling streets and iconic landmarks. The day begins with a stroll through the historic lanes of Old Delhi, where the Red Fort and Jama Masjid stand as reminders of the city's Mughal heritage. Transitioning to New Delhi, one encounters colonial-era marvels like India Gate and Rashtrapati Bhavan, reflecting the city's diverse architectural influences. Amidst this rich tapestry, Delhi's cultural scene shines bright with museums and galleries showcasing India's artistic prowess. The day culminates in a culinary adventure, exploring the city's diverse flavors from traditional eateries to trendy cafes, leaving one with a deep appreciation for Delhi's fusion of the old and the new.

Delhi, India

Enchanting Kerala: A Day of Serenity and Splendor

November 18, 2019

Embarking on a journey through Kerala, India's "God's Own Country," I began my day with a traditional breakfast before venturing into the lush hills of Munnar, where tea plantations unfolded in a breathtaking panorama. Drifting along the serene backwaters of Alleppey on a houseboat, I indulged in a seafood feast while witnessing a mesmerizing Kathakali performance later in the day. As the sun dipped below the horizon at Kovalam beach, I reflected on the day's experiences, filled with the beauty of nature, cultural richness, and warm hospitality that define Kerala.

Kerala, India

Amritsar: A Tapestry of Culture and History

May 22, 2023

Embarking on a journey through Amritsar, I am captivated by the city's rich tapestry of culture and history. At dawn, the majestic Golden Temple welcomes me with its serene ambiance, while the devout engage in prayer and reflection. The morning unfolds with moments of tranquility by the sacred sarovar, followed by a visit to Jallianwala Bagh, where the echoes of India's struggle for independence resonate deeply. The day is a testament to the resilience and spiritual fervor that define this vibrant city in the heart of Punjab.

Amritsar, India
